Music & Sound

A selection of music composing, production and sound design projects, both personal and commissioned.

Spacebarman

My main music project. I compose, record and produce all the music for Spacebarman. I have released an album and several EPs. It has recently become a live band with my colleagues Mauricio Belalcázar and Pablo Monroy.

Unfulfilled

A collection of short songs, b-sides and rejected soundtrack pieces. (2010)

El Fin de una Dictadura

A short zombie film (2010) directed by Alex Quezada. In the director's words, 'a reflection about a unpunished events of recent Colombian history'. (2017)

MixPerceptions

An Augmented-Reality art installation in which users interact with artworks with their smartphone or tablets and headphones. I created the soundtrack, sound design and programmed the audio interaction using Puredata. Finalist in the Siggraph 2014 AR contest (2014)

Mi Amigo Picú (My Friend Picú)

I designed the sound and co-composed the music (along with Daniela Torres) for this short film for children (2013)

Dunas (Dunes)

A surreal short film (2010) directed by Alex Quezada. I created the sound effects and composed the soundtrack (2010)

La Fábrica (a.k.a. Fabricamusic)

A pioneer electronic rock band from Cali, Colombia. I co-composed and produced most of the music between 2000 and 2010. The linked Bandcamp site includes a selection of works. (2000-2010)

Yo soy otro (Others)

A fantasy film by Colombian director Oscar Campo. I composed most of the original soundtrack (while working with La Fábrica). (2008)

Los Ciclos (Cycles)

A short film directed by Juan Manuel Acuña. I designed the sound effects for the short. Original score composed by Alejandro Ramírez Rojas (2007)
